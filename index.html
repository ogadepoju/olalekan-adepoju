About Me

I am an educator and researcher specializing in rhetoric, composition, and international writing pedagogy. With a PhD from the University of Louisville, my work focuses on empowering multilingual writers and decolonizing academic writing spaces through transnational approaches.

Currently serving as an Instructor at the College of Staten Island, CUNY, I design courses that recognize linguistic diversity as an asset. My research explores how writing instruction can better serve international and multilingual students.
